Bayou Fighting Tigers

The Bayou Fighting Tigers is a DCS Squadron, operating with all different kinds of Aircraft. We are welcoming towards new members and love to fly and spend time together!

Your Motivation!

What do we have to offer?

  • An active community
  • Friendly members
  • Human ATC
  • Radio-Only missions
  • Training for new players

What do we require?

  • Have DCS installed
  • Be friendly to others
  • Have fun while flying with us

Q&A

  • Q: Can I join as a DCS beginner?
    A: Totally, everyone is welcome!
  • Q: Do I need to own any morules?
    A: No official modules are required, you can fly with free mods too.
  • Q: Is there an application process?
    A: No, there is no application process.
  • Q: How do I become an AWACS or ATC?
    A: DM Shark, GabWit, Dragon or Best.
